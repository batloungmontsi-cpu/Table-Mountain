Discover Table Mountain

Table Mountain is home to more plant species than the entire United Kingdom—over 2,200 unique species!

Table Mountain is home to over 2,200 species of plants, many of which are found nowhere else on Earth. It is also part of the Cape Floral Region, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, making it not just a scenic wonder but also a biodiversity hotspot.

Wildlife includes rock hyraxes (dassies), porcupines, mongooses, and over 350 species of birds. Adventurers can enjoy abseiling, paragliding, or even cave exploring within the mountain.
